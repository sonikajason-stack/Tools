Compression Tips

  • For Web Use

    70-80% quality is usually sufficient for websites and blogs.

  • For Print

    Use 90%+ quality setting to maintain print quality.

    Often 30% smaller than JPG at the same quality level.

    Check compressed image before downloading to ensure quality.
